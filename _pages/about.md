I am a postdoctoral research fellow in the Section on Learning and Decision Making at the National Institute of Mental Health, National Institutes of Health. My research focuses on the anatomical and functional organization of large-scale forebrain circuits in primates, with particular emphasis on their roles in reinforcement learning, decision-making, and motor control.

For a complete list of my publications, please visit my [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&pli=1&user=GsupyagAAAAJ) profile.
